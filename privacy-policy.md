# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Habition handles information when you use the app.

## Summary

Habition is designed to store your habit data locally on your device. At this time, the app does not require account registration and does not send your habit data to our servers.

## Information We Process

Habition may store the following information on your device:

- Habit names and habit settings you create
- Habit completion records and related timestamps
- App preferences such as theme selection, language selection, and onboarding status

## How Information Is Stored

Your data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage provided by the app platform. This information is used only to make the app function properly and to preserve your preferences and progress between sessions.

## Data Sharing

Habition does not sell your personal information.

Habition does not share your habit data with third parties because the app currently does not transmit that data to external servers as part of its normal operation.

## Analytics and Tracking

Habition does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to track your activity inside the app.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you remove it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app, subject to how your device and operating system manage local app data.

## Children's Privacy

Habition is not knowingly designed to collect personal information from children through remote servers or online services.

## Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, protecting access to your device helps protect your information. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but we aim to minimize data exposure by keeping app data on-device where possible.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When updated, the revised version will be posted with a new effective date.
